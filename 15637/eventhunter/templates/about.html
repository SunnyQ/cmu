Popular Event Radar About

What is EventRadar?

EventRadar is a free app that helps you and your friends organize events.

When you're tired of your daily boring life and want to find some exciting events, use EventRadar to launch and check nearby events. And, when you're participating the events, you could check in!

How to use EventRadar?

It's very easy and intuitive to use EventRadar.

After you registration, you could launch your own event. Simply type in the title, description of the event, you are done. Oh wait! One last thing -- you need to give the location of your event to help us find it.
